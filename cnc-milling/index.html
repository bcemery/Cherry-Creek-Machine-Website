Our Milling Capability

Eight VMCs.
One Accountable Shop.

Cherry Creek Machine runs eight Haas vertical machining centers, ranging from the compact VF-1 to the large-format VF-4SS with Midaco pallet automation. We produce prototypes and production runs with consistent, documented results.

Renishaw on-machine probing on select VMCs catches setup errors before they become scrap. Midaco pallet automation keeps the spindle running while operators load the next job. Every finished part is inspected and documented under our ISO 9001:2015 quality system.

What Sets Our Milling Apart

Three Reasons Manufacturers
Choose Our VMCs

01
Pallet Automation — More Uptime, Faster Delivery

Our VF-4SS is equipped with a Midaco pallet system that keeps the spindle running while operators set up the next job. This means faster cycle times and better on-time delivery — not "lights-out" operation, but meaningfully more productive use of machine time.

02
Renishaw Probing Catches Errors Before They Become Scrap

Select VMCs run Renishaw on-machine probing to verify part position and critical features mid-cycle. Setups are confirmed before the part is finished — not discovered after. This is especially valuable on first-articles and tight-tolerance aerospace work.

03
One Shop, Full Size Range

From small precision components on the VF-1 to large weldment fixtures and plate work on the VF-4SS, we cover the full range under one roof. One purchase order, one point of contact, one quality system — whether your part is 2 inches or 50 inches long.
