Core Capabilities

Three Services.
One Shop.

Cherry Creek Machine runs milling, turning, and waterjet cutting under a single ISO 9001:2015 quality system. Multi-process jobs ship on a single purchase order with one point of contact and full material traceability from raw stock to finished part.

01
Precision Machining

CNC
Milling

Eight Haas vertical machining centers running 3 and 4-axis work with tolerances to ±0.0005″. From first-article prototypes to production runs of 1,000+ pieces, every job is documented and inspected under our ISO quality system. Renishaw on-machine probing catches setup errors before they become scrap. Midaco pallet automation keeps spindles running between setups.

02
Precision Machining

CNC
Turning

Five Haas lathes with live tooling, Y-axis capability, and bar feeders for efficient bar-stock-to-finished-part production. Live tooling and Y-axis eliminate secondary operations — reducing setups, lead times, and the risk of re-fixtured-part error. Up to 15″ diameter. Ideal for oil & gas components, downhole tool bodies, valve bodies, and precision shafts.

03
Cutting

Waterjet
Cutting

OMAX 80X with a 13′×7′ table — the largest waterjet in the region. Abrasive waterjet cuts virtually any material with no heat-affected zone, no metallurgical change, and no thermal stress. ±0.005″ accuracy on plate, sheet, stone, composites, and exotics. Ideal for nesting plate profiles before machining to reduce VMC cycle time.

OMAX 80X 13'×7' Table ±0.005″ Zero HAZ Any Material
Waterjet Capabilities → Get a Waterjet Quote
Key Advantages
💧
Zero Heat-Affected Zone
Cold-cutting process leaves no HAZ, no hardening, no thermal distortion. Critical for aerospace alloys, titanium, and heat-treated materials where thermal damage is not acceptable.
📐
Largest Table in the Region
13′×7′ cutting envelope handles structural plate, large weldment details, and full-sheet nesting. Parts other shops can't touch in a single cut — we can.
⚡
Pre-Machine Nesting
Nest plate profiles on the waterjet before they go to the VMC — dramatically reducing milling cycle time on complex outlines. One shop, one schedule, no outside processing.
🔩
Cuts Any Material
Aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, Inconel, composites, stone, glass, rubber, and plastics. If your drawing calls for it in plate or sheet form, waterjet can start it.
How We Work

From Quote to Ship

Every job follows the same disciplined process — from the moment your drawing lands in our inbox to the moment your parts leave with documentation in the box.

01
Drawing Review

We review your drawing for completeness, flag any questions, and confirm requirements before accepting the job. No surprises mid-run.

02
24-Hour Quote

Quote delivered within one business day. Material, quantity, tolerances, and lead time — all confirmed in writing before we start.

03
Production Planning

Work instructions created, tooling planned, inspection checkpoints defined. Fulcrum ERP job opened — your order is trackable in real time from this point forward.

04
Machining & In-Process Inspection

Parts made to print. Critical features checked mid-cycle with Renishaw probing on select VMCs. No heroics at the end — the system catches issues early.

05
Inspection, Docs & Ship

Final inspection with calibrated equipment. Dimensional results documented. Material certs pulled. Everything ships together — parts and paperwork, every time.

Industries Served

Industries Served

Cherry Creek Machine serves manufacturers across the industries that keep Oklahoma — and America — running. ISO 9001:2015 documentation meets the supply chain requirements of the most demanding sectors.

✈️
Aerospace & Defense

Structural brackets, housings, manifolds, fittings, fixture plates, bulkhead components. First-article documentation. Material traceability required — standard for us.

🛢️
Oil & Gas

Valve bodies, tool housings, adapter plates, production components, downhole tool bodies. Exotic alloys and tight tolerances are everyday work, not exceptions.

⚡
Electronics & Industrial

Enclosures, heatsinks, mounting plates, precision fixtures, custom brackets. Aluminum and copper machining for thermal management and precision assemblies.

🌾
Agriculture & Heavy Equipment

Hitch components, wear plates, structural weldment details, mounting hardware. High-volume production runs in mild steel and structural alloys.

⚙️
Industrial Equipment

Custom machine components, production tooling, fixture plates, gearbox components. Repeat production work in alloy steels and stainless.

💡
Energy & Power

Turbine components, generator housings, fluid handling components. Stainless, Inconel, and high-temperature alloys machined to print with full documentation.

🔬
Medical & Scientific

Precision components, fixture plates, lab equipment parts. Tight tolerances and clean finishes. ISO quality system supports traceability requirements.

🏗️
General Manufacturing

One-off prototypes to production runs of 1,000+ pieces. If you have a drawing and a deadline, we have the equipment and the discipline to deliver.
